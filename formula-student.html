More than just a competition

The Formula Student world has made a great before and after in my life. Thanks to it, I have grown a lot in broad areas, personally and profesionally speaking. While my jorney in this world came to and end, I keep with me all the memories, knowledge, achivements, and friends I made along the way.

Formula Student

Motorsport has always been one of my passions since I was little. Now thanks to Universitat Jaume I, I could take part in one of the most popular motorsport competitions for young talents and colleges

Joined the team in late 2023, when the UFS06 was being designed. I got in the aerodynamics department. From here, I got to work with Solidworks and StarCCMM+ to design and optimize the aero package of the formula. Later in the season, we got to manufacture the package using composites such as Carbon Fiber via Lamination. Then, in the summer of '24, we competed in the Formula Student Spain, where we faced some reliability issues and ended p14. Later we competed in Formula Student Portugal, where we took first place overall. Then for the next season, we improved in many areas inside our department, thus optimizing the design and optimization process. Then, we also managed to improve the final product, reducing the overall weight of the aero package. In the summer of 2025, the team competed in Formula Student Portugal, where we ended P3 overall, and Formula Student Spain, where we got a better result than the previous year, a bittersweet P7 overall.
