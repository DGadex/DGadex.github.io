About Me

Hi! I'm Edward Mena, a student of Videogame Design and Development at Universitat Jaume I. Thanks for visiting my portfolio website.

I’m passionate about creating interactive experiences that blend visual impact, technical precision, and meaningful design. My main interests lie in visual effects and technical design, and I aim to keep growing as a professional in the videogame industry.

I also have a deep passion for motorsport, and my dream is to one day work at a videogame studio or racing simulator developer, where I can unite both worlds I love.

Here you will find the projects I’ve worked on throughout my studies, as well as those that are still in progress. I hope you enjoy them!

Completed Projects

Factory Wars

Plants vs Zombies inspired game where the player has to defend his factory from enemies while keeping the factory running and producing resources so he can sell them and get money to pay for upgrades and defenses to keep the factory safe until he reaches his money goal. This game was made in a group with 2 other people Mario Beltrán García and Álvaro Chuan Díaz-Maroto as our final project for the Subject "Game Consoles" using DevKitPro and NightFox's Lib.

Crónicas de Castellnovo

A short graphic adventure where the player explores the town of Castellnovo to find a hidden treasure. This game was made by the Triple 7 team, where I took part in the art and several coding parts of the game. It was made for the Triple assignature project of the UJI university in 2023 and for the townhall of Castellnovo. The main goal was to promote the tourism of Castellnovo and spread the town stories.

  • View More

Comet Strike

Action videogame where the player must defeat waves of enemies while moving around the arena. This is my project for Game Engines course of UJI university in 2024. The main purpose of this game was to start scratching the surface of VFX and Shaders inside Unity, while developing a movement-focused action game.

  • Source Code

Prop Trip

Infinite fist person movement shooter where the player has to survive as long as possible while shooting enemies and dodging their bullets. This game was made by the teams Back to Bits (a team that I am part of) and Punic Goose for the UJI Game Jam 2024 in 48h. Our main goal was to explore new mechanics, their implementation challenges and dig further into the use of toon shaders and post processing effects.

  • View More

Neon Rush

Racing videogame where the player can create its own tracks with the building tool and race in them. This is my Bachelor's thesis project, where I worked in collaboration with a colleague. I took part in all the VFX and post-process along with the car behaviour and physics. We aim to keep improving the videogame.
